12 Years of Blik & Counting

Since 2002, Blik has been bringing you mouth-watering graphics. What once was just a little wall decals company in Venice has become the leading designer and maker of innovative, removable surface graphics.

Now that we’re in double digits, don’t think we’ve lost our childhood innocence. We’re still about discovery, creativity and unparalleled design. We promise to continue to amaze you with new collaborations, innovative custom work and groundbreaking graphics.
